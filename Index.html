about us

we take care of your Heart

Sri Jayadeva Institute of Cardiovascular Sciences and Research is a Government run Autonomous Institute with 1150 Beds exclusively for Cardiac care and is one of the largest single centre for heart care destination in South East Asia. This is purely a Non-Profit Organization with State of Art Cardiac Care, providing quality cardiac care at an affordable cost to all sections of the society and free of cost in deserving poor. We have effectively implemented “Treatment First – Payment Next” Concept where in the cardiac patient requiring emergency admission, shall be done without insisting for any advance payment. Institute has recorded 400% growth in last 10years.

Diseases

Common diseases include heart disease, hypertension, asthma, and diabetes. Heart disease involves blocked blood vessels, hypertension raises heart disease risk, asthma causes chronic airway inflammation, and diabetes affects blood sugar regulation.

Patient care


At our healthcare facility, we prioritize patient-centered, compassionate care. Each patient receives a personalized care plan tailored to their unique needs and preferences. Our interdisciplinary team of doctors, nurses, and specialists collaborates to ensure comprehensive, coordinated care. We utilize advanced technology for accurate diagnostics and seamless health information sharing. Our electronic health records system and telemedicine services enhance accessibility and efficiency. Compassion is at the core of our approach, with providers offering empathetic, respectful, and supportive interactions. We aim to treat every patient with dignity, addressing both physical and emotional aspects of health to promote healing and well-being. Our commitment is to deliver the highest standard of care for every patient, every time.
